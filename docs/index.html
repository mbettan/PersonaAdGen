The Pipeline

From Conversation to Broadcast-Ready

A fully automated, multi-model creative pipeline — orchestrated by ADK and driven by a single conversation with your Creative Agent.

01

Define the Persona

Talk to the Creative Agent like you would a human strategist. Share who you're reaching: their world, language, anxieties, and desires. The richer the story, the sharper the output.

Gemini 3.1 Flash
02

Upload a Base Image

Drop in a product shot, lifestyle photo, or even a rough concept image. It's your creative launchpad — the AI handles the transformation.

Your asset
03

Generate 4 Ad Scenes

Gemini 3 Pro Image renders four distinct advertising scenes — each with unique environment, mood, and a persona-matched headline — while maintaining visual consistency with your source.

04

Veo 3.1 takes each generated scene and extends it into a cinematic 8-second video with native audio and 24 FPS temporal consistency. Four scenes × 8 seconds each.
